Privacy Policy

Znap is built for sharing stories with people you choose. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have over your account.

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, username, email address, password hash, birthdate, profile photo, biography, and language preference.
  • Content you create or upload, including stories, comments, profile images, thumbnails, and optional identity verification documents.
  • Social and safety activity such as follows, blocks, reports, moderation status, and account suspension records.
  • Technical data needed to run the app, including authentication tokens, push notification tokens, time zone, device platform, and basic request metadata.

How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To show your profile, stories, comments, followers, and following lists.
  • To send notifications you enable.
  • To review reports, enforce community guidelines, prevent abuse, and support account verification.
  • To maintain, debug, and improve Znap.

Automated Content Moderation

Znap uses automated machine-learning tools to analyse images and video thumbnails you upload. This processing happens on our servers to detect content that violates our Community Guidelines (such as explicit sexual content or graphic violence). Images are not stored for training purposes and are processed solely for safety enforcement.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate Znap, such as cloud hosting, media storage, push notifications, and email delivery, or when required by law or safety obligations.

Your Controls

  • You can edit your profile, change your email or password, manage blocked users, and turn push notifications on or off in the app.
  • You can report users, stories, and comments that may violate Znap's rules.
  • You can delete your account from Settings. Account deletion removes your account and associated content from our active systems, except information we must keep for legal, security, or abuse-prevention reasons.

Children

Znap is not intended for children under 13. We ask for birthdate during registration to help enforce this requirement. If we learn that a user is under 13, we will delete their account.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact us at arturoenqzsupport@gmail.com.
